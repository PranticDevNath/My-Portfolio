Project 3 : Live Weather Website

Live weather refers to the current atmospheric conditions like temperature, humidity, wind speed, precipitation, and atmospheric pressure. This real-time data, collected from weather stations, satellites, and radar systems, is crucial for activities like agriculture, transportation, and disaster preparedness. Meteorologists analyze this information to offer accurate forecasts and updates. Accessible through websites, apps, and dedicated channels, live weather reports aid in decision-making, allowing people to plan safely for outdoor activities and mitigate risks associated with adverse weather.
